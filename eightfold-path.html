The Eightfold Path

  1. Right understanding

    2. The understanding of things as they are, a true understanding of how our actions continue beyond death and go through life without delusion and misunderstanding, to have no strong beleif and know that everything is just as intended. Everything we do in this life follows us to our next. Right understanding includes the view of what we know as "Karma".

  2. Right thought

    3. This is the view of thinking no ill will to others, to view others as equal to all life. These thoughts are beleived to help an individual helps their karma and accend to be reborn but it must of a natural willingness to devout oneself to this teaching.

  3. Right speech

    4. This teaching implores others to not fall victim to gossip, and they must not resort to abusive speech in any circumstance. Never speak of false facts and present them as facts, always make am effort to speak the truth and always speak with intent to make sure your speech is beneficial.
